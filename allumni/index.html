boundless enthusiasm and unfailing devotion to all alma mater, it's community and family.

Institute of Nutrition and Food Science (INFS) Alumni Center

Bridging Generations, Nourishing Excellence The Institute of Nutrition and Food Science (INFS) at the University of Dhaka is proud to announce the establishment of the INFS Alumni Center - a dynamic hub designed to reconnect, engage, and empower our global community of nutrition professionals and food scientists.

Our Vision

This pioneering center will serve as:

  • A professional nexus fostering lifelong connections between alumni, faculty, and students
  • An innovation incubator supporting cutting-edge research in nutrition and food science
  • A mentorship platform cultivating the next generation of industry leaders
  • A legacy project celebrating INFS's remarkable contributions to public health and nutrition

Why This Matters

For decades, INFS has been at the forefront of nutritional science in Bangladesh. Our alumni have transformed communities, shaped policies, and advanced scientific knowledge worldwide. This center will amplify that impact by:

  • Facilitating collaborative research opportunities
  • Hosting annual symposiums with industry pioneers
  • Creating scholarship programs for promising students
  • Maintaining an up-to-date professional directory

Your Invitation to Build Legacy

We invite our distinguished alumni to contribute through:

  • Financial support for infrastructure and programs
  • Knowledge sharing as guest lecturers or mentors
  • Networking opportunities for current students
  • Sponsorship of research initiatives

Building Donors

A Legacy of Generosity: The INFS Alumni Center

Where Vision Meets Commitment In 2025, the INFS Alumni Association (INFSAA) brought to life a long-cherished dream - the INFS Alumni Center. This landmark achievement stands as a testament to the extraordinary generosity of our alumni community, whose steadfast support has consistently fueled academic excellence at the Institute of Nutrition and Food Science. The Center was made possible almost entirely through the contributions of our dedicated INFSAA mem- bers - visionary donors who have made supporting INFS's core academic mission a personal commit- ment. Their collective belief in our institution's future transformed blueprints into reality, creating a dy- namic hub that will nurture generations of nutrition and food science leaders. Throughout the Center, donor recognition plaques stand as permanent reminders of this remarkable alumni solidarity. Each name represents more than financial support - it embodies a shared promise to advance nutritional science education and research in Bangladesh.Throughout the Center, donor recog- nition plaques stand as permanent reminders of this remarkable alumni solidarity. To Our Donors:

Your philanthropy has built more than a building. You've created:

  • A home for innovation where students and researchers collaborate
  • A bridge connecting INFS's proud history to its ambitious future
  • A living testament to the power of alumni unity

As we celebrate this milestone, we renew our commitment to honoring your trust by ensuring the Center becomes everything you envisioned - and more. "What we build together, endures together."
