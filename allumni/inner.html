We are........

The Institute of Nutrition and Food Science Alumni Association (INFSAA) is a non-political, non-aligned, and voluntary learned society that brings together the alumni and faculty of the Institute of Nutrition and Food Science (INFS), University of Dhaka. Established in 2012 during a memorable reunion, INFSAA was founded by a group of visionary alumni who recognized the need for a dedicated platform to foster lifelong connections, professional collaborations, and knowledge exchange.

Over the years, INFSAA has evolved into a vibrant network of nutritionists, food scientists, public health professionals, researchers, and industry leaders, all united by a shared passion for advancing nutrition science and promoting healthier communities. Our mission is to strengthen the bond among alumni, support academic and professional growth, and contribute to national and global efforts in nutrition, food security, and public health.

We have a few core values and ideals that guide us in our path of service toward each other and the alumni community.

We strive to be a safe, fun, open and innovative diverse community that welcomes individuals from all backgrounds and encourages them to authentically themselves, feel that they belong, believe they can contribute meaningfully.

Excellence

We strive to be outstanding in all that we do, both as individuals and as a team. We collectively embrace a growth mindset focused on learning and continuous improvement.

Innovation

Fun
